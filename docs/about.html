About Elite Tax & Finance

Your trusted partner for comprehensive tax and financial solutions

17+ Years Experience
Since 2008

With over 17 years of dedicated experience in tax preparation and financial services, our leader Levell Williams has been helping individuals and businesses navigate the complexities of tax compliance since 2008.

His expertise spans the full spectrum of tax preparation, from straightforward individual returns to highly complex business filings across all entity types, including sole proprietorships, partnerships, and corporations.

We understand that every client's tax situation is unique, which is why we take a personalized approach to ensure accuracy, maximize deductions, and minimize tax liability. Beyond tax preparation, we offer comprehensive financial services including strategic tax planning, professional bookkeeping, internal audit preparation, and ongoing tax strategy consultation to help clients make informed decisions year-round.

Whether you're an individual seeking to optimize your personal tax situation or a business owner managing complex financial structures, we're committed to providing reliable, professional service backed by nearly two decades of experience. Our goal is to take the stress out of tax season while ensuring you remain compliant and financially strategic throughout the year.
